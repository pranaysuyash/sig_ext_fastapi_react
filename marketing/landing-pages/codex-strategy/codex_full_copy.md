## 7. FAQ

1. **Does everything run locally?** — Yes. All extraction and signing happens on-device by default. Optional cloud sync is available for teams with regional storage requirements.
2. **What files can I import?** — Drop in PDFs, PNG, JPG, TIFF, or HEIC up to 500MB. We auto-handle EXIF orientation and DPI normalization.
3. **How do teams collaborate?** — Pro and Team tiers sync signature outputs via encrypted workspaces. Enterprise offers on-prem deployment with SSO.
4. **Do you provide audit logs?** — Every export can include a JSONL audit log with timestamps, operators, and signature hashes. Export them to your compliance vault automatically.
5. **What’s the pricing model?** — Free tier covers unlimited local extractions. Pro unlocks cloud sync and batch processing. Team tier adds API access; Enterprise is custom.
6. **Need to talk to a human?** — Email support@signkit.work or join the Slack community. We reply within one business day.
