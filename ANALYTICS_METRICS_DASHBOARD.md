## ðŸŽ“ METRIC DEFINITIONS

### **Bounce Rate**
Percentage of visitors who leave after viewing only one page.
- Good: <60%
- Average: 60-70%
- Poor: >70%

### **Average Session Duration**
Average time visitors spend on site per session.
- Good: >2 minutes
- Average: 1-2 minutes
- Poor: <1 minute

### **Pages per Session**
Average number of pages viewed per session.
- Good: >2.5
- Average: 1.5-2.5
- Poor: <1.5

### **Conversion Rate**
Percentage of visitors who make a purchase.
- Desktop software average: 2-4%
- SignKit target: 2.5-4%

### **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**
Total marketing spend divided by new customers acquired.
- For $29 product: Target <$20 (30% of LTV)
- For profitability: CAC should be <50% of price

### **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)**
Revenue generated per dollar spent on ads.
- Formula: Ad Revenue / Ad Spend
- Minimum acceptable: 2:1
- Good: 3-5:1
- Great: >5:1

### **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**
Likelihood of customer recommending product.
- Calculation: % Promoters (9-10) - % Detractors (0-6)
- Poor: <0
- Average: 0-30
- Good: 30-50
- Excellent: >50
