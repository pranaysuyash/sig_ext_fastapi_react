# Competitive Analysis & Market Research

## Executive Summary

The signature extraction and document signing market is dominated by cloud-based SaaS solutions (DocuSign, Adobe Sign) with pricing starting at $10-40/month per user. Our desktop-first, privacy-focused approach with one-time/annual licensing represents a unique positioning in an underserved niche.

**Key Findings:**
- **Market Gap**: No major desktop-first signature extraction tool with local processing
- **Pricing Opportunity**: Our $29-79/year pricing is 60-80% cheaper than competitors
- **Privacy Advantage**: Local processing appeals to privacy-conscious users and regulated industries
- **Feature Parity**: We can match 80% of competitor features at 20% of the cost
