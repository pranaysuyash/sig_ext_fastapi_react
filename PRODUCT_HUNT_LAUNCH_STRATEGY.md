## 1. Product Hunt Launch Best Practices

### Optimal Launch Day & Time

**Best Time:**
- **12:01 AM - 1:00 AM PST** (Product Hunt operates on Pacific Time)
- Product Hunt resets at midnight PST, giving you a full 24-hour window
- Launching at 12:01 AM PST ensures maximum exposure throughout the entire day

**Best Day:**
- **For Maximum Traffic:** Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday
  - Highest engagement and traffic
  - Most competitive days
  - Best for products with strong existing networks

- **For Top Rankings:** Monday or Friday
  - Lower competition
  - Better chance at top spots with fewer upvotes
  - Still maintain good engagement

- **Avoid:** Weekends and holidays (significantly lower engagement)

**Critical Note:** The best day is ultimately when you're most prepared with your community mobilized.

---

### How to Maximize Upvotes

#### The Algorithm & Ranking System

Product Hunt's ranking is determined by:
1. **Upvote Quantity** - Total number of upvotes
2. **Upvote Weight** - Votes from established, active PH members count more
3. **Upvote Velocity** - Rate of upvotes over time (25-50 votes/hour is ideal)
4. **Engagement** - Comments and discussions boost visibility
5. **User Quality** - Long-standing accounts have more weight than new accounts

**Target Benchmarks:**
- **First Hour:** 30-100 upvotes (critical momentum building)
- **First 4 Hours:** Get into top 4 positions (random rotation period ends at 4am PST)
- **Product of the Day:** ~1,000+ upvotes (high confidence level)
- **Top 5:** 300-500 upvotes typically sufficient

#### Maximizing Strategies

**Pre-Launch (3-4 months before):**
- Create Product Hunt account early (algorithm favors established accounts)
- Engage authentically: upvote products, leave thoughtful comments, participate in discussions
- Build relationships with top hunters and community members
- Ask your network to create PH accounts 30 days before launch

**Pre-Launch (3-4 weeks before):**
- Email your list to create PH accounts and start engaging
- Send follow-up email 3 days later asking them to spend 2 minutes upvoting/commenting on other products
- This "warms up" their accounts - votes from active accounts count significantly more

**Launch Day:**
- Mobilize your network across multiple channels simultaneously
- Spread engagement throughout the day to maintain velocity
- First 4 hours are CRITICAL - aim for top 4 position
- Last 2 hours also crucial for final push

**What NOT to Do:**
- ‚ùå Ask directly for upvotes (violates PH Terms of Service)
- ‚ùå Mass message users
- ‚ùå Use bots or automation
- ‚ùå Incentivize upvotes with rewards
- ‚ùå Create fake accounts
- ‚ùå Have sudden spikes (looks suspicious)

### Hunter vs Maker Launch

**Current Best Practice (2025):** Makers should hunt their own products.

**Why the Change:**
- Product Hunt used to email hunter's followers, making popular hunters valuable
- This mechanism no longer provides the same advantage
- Manual homepage curation is now more important than hunter's follower count
- No discernible advantage to using third-party hunter

**When a Hunter Might Help:**
- You have zero Product Hunt presence
- The hunter has genuine relationships in your target community
- They can provide strategic launch advice
- They'll actively engage throughout launch day

**Bottom Line:** If you've built a solid pre-launch list, you don't need a hunter.

### Community Engagement Tactics

#### Active Engagement Strategy

**Launch Day Engagement:**
- Respond to EVERY comment within minutes
- Be present and active throughout the entire day
- Thank early supporters genuinely
- Answer questions thoroughly and helpfully
- Keep conversations going to boost visibility
- Be humble and helpful, not promotional

**First Comment Strategy:**
- YOU should post the first comment (as the maker)
- Make it engaging and discussion-worthy
- Include: brief intro, problem you're solving, why you built it
- End with a thoughtful question to spark discussion
- Mention Product Hunt exclusive offer if applicable

**Comment Response Best Practices:**
- Acknowledge specific points in their comment
- Provide additional context or examples
- Ask follow-up questions
- Show genuine appreciation
- Keep it conversational, not corporate

**Velocity Management:**
- Aim for 25-50 upvotes per hour (steady pace)
- Comments and discussions are as important as upvotes
- Products with comments rank higher than those without
- Active discussion signals genuine interest to the algorithm

#### Multi-Channel Mobilization

**Email:**
- Send to supporters in waves throughout the day (5-6 waves)
- Personalize for different segments
- Never ask for upvotes directly - ask for "support" and "feedback"
- Include clear CTA button with Product Hunt link

**Social Media:**
- Twitter/X: Multiple posts throughout the day
- LinkedIn: Professional audience, emphasize productivity benefits
- Facebook/Slack groups: Relevant communities only (avoid spam)
- Ask team members to share from their accounts

**Direct Outreach:**
- Partners who agreed to support
- Maker friends in the community
- Previous customers/users
- Industry influencers (personalized messages)

**Important:** Ask for "support" and "feedback," never explicitly for "upvotes."
