# SignKit Revenue Forecast Model 2025-2026

**Document Created:** November 15, 2025
**Product:** SignKit - Desktop Signature Extraction & PDF Signing Tool
**Pricing:** $29 (Launch) / $39 (Regular) Lifetime License
**Market:** B2B Professionals (Legal, Real Estate, Design, Finance)

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This forecast presents three scenarios (Conservative, Moderate, Aggressive) for SignKit's revenue trajectory from launch through December 2026. Based on market research, competitor analysis, and indie SaaS benchmarks, the **Moderate scenario** targeting **$3,500-5,000 MRR by end of 2026** is most realistic for a bootstrapped solo founder executing a comprehensive marketing strategy.

**Key Assumptions:**
- Launch Price: $29 for first 500 customers
- Regular Price: $39 thereafter
- Target Market: 74% B2B professionals working remotely (desktop preference 68%)
- No subscriptions initially (pure lifetime model)
- Gumroad as primary sales platform (10% platform fee)

**PDF-Related Search Activity:**

## REVENUE MODEL ASSUMPTIONS

### Pricing Strategy

**Phase 1: Launch Pricing (First 500 customers)**
- Price: $29 lifetime license
- Psychology: Charm pricing ($29 vs $30) increases conversion 5-7%
- Urgency: Limited "founding member" pricing creates FOMO
- Duration: Estimated 3-6 months to sell 500 units

**Phase 2: Regular Pricing (Customer 501+)**
- Price: $39 lifetime license
- Positioning: Still 85% cheaper than Adobe annual cost
- Value prop: "Own it forever vs. $240/year subscriptions"

**Phase 3: Pro Tier (6-12 months post-launch)**
- Price: $15/mo or $129/year
- Features: Multi-user sync, batch processing, browser extension
- Target: 10-15% of existing users upgrade
- New revenue stream begins Month 9-12

### Platform Fees & Costs

**Gumroad (Primary Sales Channel):**
- Platform fee: 10% of gross revenue
- Payment processing: Included in 10%
- Payout: Instant (with optional express fee) or standard
- Conversion rate assumption: 3-5% (e-commerce median 4.2%)

**Operating Costs (Monthly):**
- Domain/hosting: $20/month
- Marketing tools: $50-100/month (email, analytics)
- Total fixed costs: ~$100/month Year 1

## SENSITIVITY ANALYSIS

### Impact of Price Changes

**If Launch Price = $39 (no $29 early bird):**
- Moderate Scenario Month 1: 90 units (25% fewer) = $3,159 net
- Trade-off: Higher revenue per customer but slower initial adoption
- Recommendation: **Stick with $29 launch price** for momentum

**If Regular Price = $49 instead of $39:**
- Moderate Scenario total revenue: +20% ($96,794 vs $80,662)
- Risk: Conversion rate may drop 10-15% at $49
- Recommendation: **Test $49 in Month 8-9** with A/B testing

### Impact of Gumroad Alternatives

**Switching to Stripe (2.9% + $0.30):**
- Month with $10,000 gross revenue:
  - Gumroad (10%): $9,000 net
  - Stripe: $9,700 - ($290 + $11.70) = $9,398 net
  - Savings: $398/month at $10k gross
- **Recommendation:** Switch to Stripe when monthly gross > $5,000 (Month 7-8)

### Impact of Marketing Spend

**Adding $500/mo Paid Ads (Months 3-14):**
- Cost: $6,000 over 12 months
- Required additional sales: 154 units @ $39 = $6,006
- Needed conversion improvement: 13 extra sales/month
- **Recommendation:** Test with $200/mo in Month 6, scale if CAC < $30

## RISK FACTORS & MITIGATION

### Market Risks

**Risk 1: Low search volume for "signature extraction"**
- Mitigation: Target broader keywords ("pdf signature tool", "extract signature from image")
- Backup: Build comparison pages ("SignKit vs Adobe", "SignKit vs DocuSign")

**Risk 2: Competitors launch similar tools**
- Mitigation: First-mover advantage + privacy-first positioning
- Backup: Add unique features (batch processing, audit logs) faster

**Risk 3: Adobe/DocuSign adds extraction features**
- Mitigation: Price advantage ($29-39 vs $120-240/year) remains
- Backup: Pivot to "lightweight alternative" messaging

**Risk 1: Product Hunt launch flops (<50 upvotes)**

## APPENDIX: CALCULATION METHODOLOGIES

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Estimates

**Organic Channels (Month 1-6):**
- Time investment: 20 hrs/week × $0/hr = $0 CAC
- Reality: Opportunity cost exists but not cash cost

**Paid Channels (if added Month 6+):**
- Google Ads CPC: $5-8 for "pdf signature tool"
- Landing page conversion: 4%
- CAC = ($6.50 CPC ÷ 4%) = $162.50 per customer
- LTV: $39 lifetime + potential $180 Pro upgrade = $219
- **LTV:CAC = 1.35** (marginal, avoid paid ads initially)

### Churn Assumptions

**Lifetime Model:**
- No churn possible (one-time payment)
- Risk: No repeat revenue (mitigated by Pro tier)

**Pro Tier:**
- Expected annual churn: 5-7% (below SaaS median of 3.5% monthly)
- Reasoning: Users who pay for Pro are power users
- Mitigation: Annual billing reduces monthly churn psychology

### Market Penetration Estimates

**Total Addressable Users (TAM):**
- 73% of B2B businesses use webinars (signature extraction pain point)
- ~10M knowledge workers globally in legal, real estate, finance
- If 0.1% need signature extraction = 10,000 potential customers

**Realistic Market Share:**
- Moderate scenario: 2,224 customers = 0.02% of TAM
