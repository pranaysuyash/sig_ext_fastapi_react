Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the product

How is this different from Adobe Acrobat?

Signature Extractor is designed specifically for signature extraction with advanced controls, while Adobe Acrobat is a general PDF tool. Our desktop-first approach with privacy by design means your documents never leave your computer, unlike Adobe's cloud integration.

Is my data secure with local processing?

Absolutely! All processing happens locally on your machine. Your documents never leave your computer, ensuring complete privacy and security. We don't collect or store any of your data.

What file formats do you support?

We support common image formats (PNG, JPG, JPEG, TIFF) and PDF documents. For output, you can save as transparent PNG, JPEG, or place directly in PDF documents.

Can I use this for commercial purposes?

Yes! The lifetime license allows you to use Signature Extractor for both personal and commercial purposes. You can process documents for clients, create templates, and integrate into your business workflows.

How does the PDF signing feature work?

Our PDF integration allows you to open PDF documents, place signatures from your library directly onto PDF pages, and save the signed document. This creates a properly signed PDF that can be used for official purposes.
