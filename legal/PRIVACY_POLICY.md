# Privacy Policy for Signature Extractor

**Last Updated**: November 7, 2025
**Effective Date**: November 7, 2025

## 1. Overview

Signature Extractor is a desktop application designed to extract signatures from images and documents. This privacy policy explains how we handle, collect, and protect your information when you use our application.

**Our Commitment**: We prioritize your privacy by processing all data locally on your device whenever possible.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information We DON'T Collect

Signature Extractor is designed to work **offline-first** and does **NOT** collect:

- ❌ Your signature images or documents
- ❌ Personal files or data stored on your computer
- ❌ Location information
- ❌ Usage patterns or analytics data (unless explicitly enabled)
- ❌ Personal identifiers without your consent

### 2.2 Information We May Collect (Minimal)

**License Information**:
- Email address (when you activate a license)
- License key for product activation
- Purchase information from Gumroad (processed by Gumroad)

**Optional Crash Reports** (if enabled):
- Application error logs
- System information (OS version, hardware specs)
- No personal data or file contents

**Support Communications**:
- Information you provide when contacting support
- Email address for support responses

## 3. How We Process Your Data

### 3.1 Local Processing (Default)

- **All signature extraction** happens locally on your device
- **No images or documents** are uploaded to our servers
- **No cloud processing** unless you explicitly enable cloud features
- **Your files remain private** on your computer

### 3.2 Optional Cloud Features

Some features may require internet connectivity:

- **License Validation**: Validates your license key online
- **Update Checks**: Checks for application updates
- **Support**: Communications with our support team

### 3.3 Third-Party Services

**Gumroad** (Payment Processing):
- Handles payment processing and license key delivery
- Their privacy policy applies to purchase transactions
- We do not receive your payment information

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

- **License Information**: Stored locally on your device at `~/.signature_extractor/license.json`
- **Temporary Files**: Created during processing and automatically deleted
- **No sensitive data** is stored in plain text unnecessarily

### 4.2 Security Measures

- **Local Processing**: Reduces data exposure risks
- **Secure License Storage**: Encrypted license information
- **No Data Collection**: Minimal data collection reduces attack surface
- **Regular Updates**: Security patches delivered through updates
