**Your price point ($29) is in a sweet spot where pay-first often works well.**

## üö® THE REAL RISKS OF FREE DOWNLOAD

### **1. Never-Pay Problem**

- People download and use trial forever
- 70-80% of free users never convert
- You're giving away the core product

### **2. Piracy Risk**

- App can be shared/distributed
- No technical protection (desktop app)
- License key system can be bypassed

### **3. Support Burden**

- 10x more users to support
- Most are free users who never pay
- Support costs go up, revenue might not

### **4. Competitive Analysis**

- Your app can be reverse-engineered
- Competitors can see your features
- Trade secrets exposed

---

## ‚úÖ WHEN FREE DOWNLOAD WORKS

### **1. Complex/Professional Software**

- Users need time to evaluate
- High learning curve
- High price point ($99+)

### **2. Feature-Rich Applications**

- Lots of features to explore
- Long evaluation process
- User needs to understand value

### **3. Subscription Models**

- Ongoing revenue from free users
- LTV (lifetime value) justifies acquisition cost
- Free users might upgrade later

## ‚ùå WHEN PAY-FIRST WORKS BETTER
