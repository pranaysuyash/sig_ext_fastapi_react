**Market Validation**:
- 4+ billion email users worldwide
- Professionals send 40-50 emails/day
- Email signature = 40-50 brand impressions/day
- Generic tools are ugly and don't leverage signatures

**Competitive Advantage**:
- Only tool that starts with extracted signature
- Auto-color matching (no manual design needed)
- Signature-first layouts (signature is the hero)
- One-click multi-platform export

- Every email you send shows SignKit-generated signature

**Implementation Simplicity**:

- Export scripts (one-time per platform)
- No backend required (all local)

**Monetization**:

- Enterprise ($299): Custom branding + team templates
