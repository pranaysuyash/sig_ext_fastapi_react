## Exceptions (Rare)

### When We WOULD Refund

**1. Technical Issues We Can't Fix**
- App doesn't work on their system
- We've tried everything
- It's genuinely broken for them
- Refund as exception

**2. Fraudulent Purchase**
- Credit card fraud
- Unauthorized purchase
- Payment error
- Refund immediately

**3. Our Mistake**
- We messed up somehow
- Wrong price charged
- License not delivered
- Make it right

**These are rare and legitimate.**

### When We WOULDN'T Refund

**1. Changed Mind**
- "I don't need it anymore"
- "I found a free alternative"
- "It's not what I expected"
- **Response**: You tried it first

**2. Buyer's Remorse**
- "I shouldn't have bought it"
- "I can't afford it"
- "I made a mistake"
- **Response**: Trial was available

**3. Completed Use**
- "I extracted all my signatures"
- "I finished my project"
- "I don't need it anymore"
- **Response**: That's what you paid for

**4. Feature Requests**
- "It doesn't do X"
- "I want Y feature"
- "It should work differently"
- **Response**: You saw what it does
