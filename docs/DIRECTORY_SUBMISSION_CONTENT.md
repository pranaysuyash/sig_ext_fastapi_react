SignKit is a privacy-first desktop application designed for professionals, freelancers, and small businesses who need to extract signatures from documents and sign PDFs without compromising their privacy or paying recurring subscription fees.

**What Makes SignKit Different**

Unlike cloud-based e-signature platforms like DocuSign or Adobe Sign, SignKit runs entirely on your computer. There's no monthly subscription‚Äîjust a one-time $29 purchase that you own forever.

**Core Features**

SignKit's signature extraction capability is unique in the market. Upload any image or scanned document containing a signature, use the intuitive selection tool to isolate the signature region, adjust the threshold and color settings, and export a clean, transparent PNG ready to use anywhere. The app includes advanced image processing controls including zoom, pan, rotation, and morphological operations to ensure perfect extraction every time.

The integrated PDF signing workflow lets you open any PDF document, select signatures from your library, place them precisely where needed with drag-and-drop positioning and resizing, and save the signed PDF‚Äîall without uploading to the cloud. The app maintains a comprehensive audit log of all PDF operations for compliance and record-keeping.

**Privacy & Security**

SignKit is built with privacy as the foundation. All image processing happens locally using OpenCV and PIL. Your files are never uploaded to external servers. The app works completely offline after installation, requiring no internet connection for core functionality. This makes it GDPR and HIPAA friendly, perfect for regulated industries.

**Technical Excellence**

Built with Python and PySide6 (Qt), SignKit delivers a native desktop experience on macOS, Windows, and Linux. The hybrid architecture includes an optional local FastAPI backend for advanced features, but degrades gracefully if unavailable. The app includes multi-layer security validation, secure file handling, and automatic cleanup of temporary files.

SignKit costs $29 as a one-time purchase with no recurring fees. The trial version includes full functionality except export and save operations, allowing you to test thoroughly before buying. Available for macOS (Intel and Apple Silicon), Windows 10/11, and Linux (Ubuntu, Fedora).
