## ðŸ’¬ FAQ Responses

**Q: Will my price increase if I buy now?**
A: No! One-time payment means you pay once and own it forever. Your price never increases.

**Q: Why are you increasing the price?**
A: We're continuously improving SignKit with new features and updates. The price reflects the growing value. Early adopters get rewarded with the lowest price.

**Q: Will there be sales or discounts?**
A: We don't do fake sales. The price increases gradually and predictably. The best time to buy is always now.

**Q: What if I wait until the price is higher?**
A: You'll pay more, but you'll still get the same great product with all future updates included.
