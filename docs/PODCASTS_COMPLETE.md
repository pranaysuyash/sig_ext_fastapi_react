## üé§ AUDIO SETUP RECOMMENDATIONS

### Minimum Setup:
- **Mic:** Built-in laptop mic (acceptable for most podcasts)
- **Headphones:** Any headphones to prevent echo
- **Software:** Zoom, Riverside.fm, or SquadCast (host provides)
- **Internet:** Stable connection (wired preferred)

### Recommended Setup ($100-200):
- **Mic:** Blue Yeti or Audio-Technica ATR2100x
- **Headphones:** Any comfortable over-ear headphones
- **Pop Filter:** Reduces plosives ($10-20)
- **Boom Arm:** Keeps mic positioned ($20-40)

### Professional Setup ($500+):
- **Mic:** Shure SM7B or Rode PodMic
- **Interface:** Focusrite Scarlett 2i2
- **Headphones:** Audio-Technica ATH-M50x
- **Acoustic Treatment:** Foam panels for room

**Note:** Most podcasts don't require professional setup. Good content > perfect audio.
