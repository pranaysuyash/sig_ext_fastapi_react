## üí∞ Pricing Strategy

**Launch Pricing:**
- $29 lifetime license (launch promotion)
- $39 lifetime license (post-launch standard)
- No subscription model initially

**Rationale:**
- Low barrier to entry
- Viral potential (people share good deals)
- Builds customer base quickly
- Can introduce subscription tier later for advanced features

## üìà Unit Economics

**Target Metrics:**
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): $10-30
- Lifetime Value (LTV): $29-39
- LTV:CAC Ratio: 1.5-3.0x
- Payback Period: Immediate (one-time purchase)

**Channel Economics:**
- SEO/Content: $0-5 CAC (mostly time investment)
- Product Hunt: $5-15 CAC
- Reddit: $0-10 CAC (organic engagement)
- Bing Ads: $15-25 CAC
- Retargeting: $10-20 CAC
- Affiliates: $8-12 CAC (30% commission)

## üéØ Growth Levers

1. **Organic Traffic** (SEO/Content) - Highest margin, longest lag
2. **Product Hunt Launch** - One-time spike, builds momentum
3. **Affiliate Program** - Scalable, performance-based
4. **Retargeting Ads** - High conversion, limited scale
5. **Bing Ads** - Profitable at scale, B2B audience
