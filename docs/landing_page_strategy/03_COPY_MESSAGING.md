## Micro-Copy Guidelines

### Button Text

- Use action verbs: "Get", "Buy", "Download", "Extract"
- Keep under 4 words when possible
- Make the benefit clear: "Lifetime Access" vs "Buy Now"

### Form Labels

- Clear and descriptive: "Email address" not "Email"
- Specify what happens: "Get download link" vs "Submit"

### Tooltips and Help Text

- Anticipate questions: "No credit card required"
- Set expectations: "Average download time: 30 seconds"

### Error Messages

- Be helpful, not scary: "Something went wrong. Please try again."
- Provide next steps: "If problems persist, email support@..."
